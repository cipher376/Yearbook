Welcome to Fame

Create, track, find and attend all events around you. Publish your personal events and invite people to attend.

What is fame?

Fame is an app intended to help you advertise your events to the world for free. Let your wedding, funeral and other gatherings known to million of users. Don't miss out your friends birthday, use the reminder to keep track of your booked events.

Social life

Make new friends and connect with people who have booked for events you are interested in. Chat with your new friends or join the event public room. Comment, like, dislike and share events with your friends.

The digital tickets!

Are you an event organizer? If yes then fame is for you, just create electronic tickets and sell them online with just a few clicks.

And...! the news

Don't miss the juicy gossips about the last event you attended. Its all live on fame news.

Don't miss your way!

Fame is to remind you and give you the location of that important events you really don't want to miss. Find that wedding, funeral, birthday party on google map that is happening live in your vicinity.

How do I get started?

Its so easy, just register and start booking for events but don't forget to complete your profile if you want to stay connected with friends. That's it.
